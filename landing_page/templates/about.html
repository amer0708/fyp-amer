About Us

Waniey J. Exclusive Tailor was established in 2018. We provide customers with the finest suits, blazers, batiks, and other outfit collections ready to wear. Our company specializes in corporate uniforms, batiks, and tailor-made services. We offer mass production uniforms as well as bespoke tailoring for unique styles and designs.

With a wide selection of high-quality materials, we ensure our clothing is not only stylish but also durable and comfortable. Whether for everyday business wear, formal events, or custom designs for special occasions, we are dedicated to providing clothing that meets the highest standards of quality.
